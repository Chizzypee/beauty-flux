Who We Are

About the MISS BEAUTY FLUX oganization. We are the Number 1 Nigerian company in the international beauty pageant industry . We are exclusive representative in Nigeria of the most important international beauty competitions

The MISS BEAUTY FLUX Organization

We have 19 years of national amd international experience in event production, beauty contests and modelling.

A company with Qualified National Directors in more than 100 countries. Their responsibility is to apply in each county the international promotional plan of the host country, host city, MISS BEAUTY FLUX and its partners, Our National Directors also select and prepare the contestant who will represent their country in the MISS BEAUTY FLUX international beauty contest.

We are the first Organization in teh beauty pageant industry who decided to promote the usage of cryptocurrencies. We decided to show to the entire world that no matter how big are the cost of the production, the cryptocurrency can be used like any flat currency (US dollars, euro,etc) to cover all the production costs. We believe that the cryptocurrencies are the future of payment and the financial transactions.
